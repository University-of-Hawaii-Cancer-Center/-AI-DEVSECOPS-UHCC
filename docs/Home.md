# 🚀 Welcome to the CICD-AI-UHCC Wiki

The **CICD-AI-UHCC** repository represents the bleeding-edge of automated ML Ops and AI Continuous Integration tailored specifically for the University of Hawaii Cancer Center (UHCC).

## 🛡️ Executive Mission
As AI Agents and autonomous pipelines become ubiquitous, this framework guarantees that all analytical processing strictly adheres to **UH Executive Policy 2.214 (Security and Protection of Sensitive Information)**, NIST 800-53, and NSPM-33. Our architecture fundamentally hardens research pipelines, preserving grant funding and protecting the university from cyber-threats.

## 📚 Wiki Directory
Please explore the following documentation pages to deeply understand our governance, architecture, and operational procedures:

1. **[EP 2.214 AI Agent Compliance](./EP-2.214-Compliance.md)**: Rules of engagement for AI Agents, LLMs, and autonomous systems interacting with UH Institutional Data.
2. **[Data Classification & Risk Tiers](./Data-Classification.md)**: A deep dive into the four data tiers (Public, Restricted, Sensitive, Regulated) and where they can be processed.
3. **[Automated ML Ops Architecture](./ML-Ops-Architecture.md)**: Diagrams and workflow documentation for the CI/CD pipeline (Linting, Security Scanning, Deployment).
